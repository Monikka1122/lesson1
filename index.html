About Us

History

In the early 2020s, Nix's love for animals led her to start Pet Sitting, a business born out of her desire to provide excellent care for pets while their owners traveled. Her commitment to exceptional service and creating a loving environment quickly made her business a local favorite. Over the years, Pet Sitting expanded its services to include dog walking, pet transportation, and personalized playdates, becoming a trusted lifeline for pet owners. Today, it continues to thrive, showcasing how a simple idea founded on love can transform into a successful and rewarding business.
